About

Bleeding Serpent is a personal project, a space for the curious, the seekers, and the wanderers of life, life both seen and unseen. We will journey through mysticism, occultism, and the mysteries beyond the ordinary, while also embracing practical, science-backed knowledge and tools for personal growth, health and longevity. This is a place to expand your mind, challenge your perspectives, and cultivate a deeper understanding of yourself and the world.
